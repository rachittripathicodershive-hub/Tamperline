This Cookie Policy explains how Tamperline.us uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website or use our brewery inventory and booking platform. By continuing to use Tamperline.us, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

01

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, to remember your preferences, and to provide information to the site owners. Cookies do not contain executable code or viruses — they are plain text data files read by your browser.

Similar technologies such as web beacons, pixel tags, and local storage may also be used alongside cookies to provide a consistent and personalised experience on Tamperline.us.

02

Types of Cookies We Use

Tamperline.us uses the following categories of cookies:

  • Essential cookies — required for the platform to function correctly
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how the platform is used
  • Preference cookies — remember your settings and customisations
  • Third-party cookies — set by external services we integrate with
ℹ️ We do not use advertising or tracking cookies designed to build a profile for marketing purposes. Your brewery data is never used for ad targeting.
03

Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for Tamperline.us to operate. Without them, features like login sessions, dashboard access, and booking management cannot function. Because they are essential, they cannot be disabled.

  • Session token — keeps you securely logged in during your session
  • CSRF token — protects against cross-site request forgery attacks
  • Auth state — identifies whether you are authenticated as a brewery operator or staff member
  • Cookie consent — records your cookie preferences so we don't ask again
04

Analytics Cookies

Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors use Tamperline.us so we can improve the platform experience for brewery operators. The data collected is aggregated and anonymised — it cannot be used to identify you personally.

  • Pages visited and time spent on each section
  • Features used most frequently within the dashboard
  • Error or crash reports to improve stability
  • Device type and browser version for compatibility testing

You may opt out of analytics cookies at any time through your account settings or by using your browser's cookie controls.

05

Preference Cookies

Preference cookies remember choices you have made on Tamperline.us so you do not have to re-enter them on each visit. These include:

  • Dashboard layout and column configurations
  • Default date ranges for inventory reports
  • Notification and alert preferences
  • Language and regional settings (e.g., date format, units of measure)

Preference cookies expire after 90 days of inactivity. You can clear them at any time from Settings → Account Preferences.

06

Third-Party Cookies

Some features on Tamperline.us rely on third-party services that may set their own cookies. We only integrate trusted providers necessary for platform operation:

  • Stripe — payment processing for subscription billing; sets cookies for fraud prevention and session continuity
  • Google Fonts — serves typography assets; may set a session cookie for font delivery optimisation
  • Cloudflare — CDN and DDoS protection; sets security and performance cookies
⚠️ Third-party cookies are governed by each provider's own privacy policy. We are not responsible for the content of third-party cookie policies. Please review their policies directly for full details.
07

Managing Cookies

You have full control over non-essential cookies. You can manage or delete cookies through your web browser settings. Here is how to do so in common browsers:

  • Chrome — Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox — Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari — Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge — Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

Please note that disabling essential cookies will prevent you from logging in and using core platform features. Disabling preference cookies means your dashboard settings will not be saved between sessions.

08

Policy Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, regulation, or our platform. When we make material changes we will notify active users via email and display a notice on the Tamperline.us dashboard at least 14 days before the changes take effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page always reflects the most recent revision.
