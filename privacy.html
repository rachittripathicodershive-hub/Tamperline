This Privacy Policy explains how Tamperline.us LLC ("Tamperline", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when you use our website, brewery management platform, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

This policy also explains your privacy rights and how applicable data protection laws protect you, including: GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable global privacy and data protection regulations.

2. Personal Data We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Business or company information
  • Login credentials
  • Billing and subscription details
  • Customer support communications
  • Data uploaded or entered into the platform

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address
  • Approximate geographic location
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Usage activity and feature interaction logs
  • Access timestamps
  • Cookies and tracking identifiers

2.3 Payment Information

Tamperline.us does not store or process payment card details directly. Payments are securely processed by certified third-party payment providers such as Wise, Stripe, and Paddle.

We may receive limited transaction information including payment status, subscription plan, transaction ID, and billing period for billing, subscription management, and financial recordkeeping.

03

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Where GDPR applies, we process personal data under the following lawful bases:

  • Contractual Necessity: To provide and operate the Services you request
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our platform, prevent fraud, and ensure security
  • Legal Obligations: To comply with financial, tax, or regulatory requirements
  • User Consent: Where required for certain features or marketing communications
04

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Services
  • Manage user accounts and authentication
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send service notifications and account updates
  • Provide system alerts such as low-stock alerts and booking confirmations
  • Provide customer support
  • Detect fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access
  • Improve platform performance and user experience
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
05

5. Customer Data in the Platform (CRM Data)

Tamperline.us acts as a data processor for operational data entered into the platform.

This may include:

  • Brewing recipes
  • Batch logs
  • Inventory records
  • Supplier information
  • Customer bookings
  • Production schedules

We treat all such data as confidential. Tamperline.us does not sell or share proprietary production data with other users or third parties except where required by law.

Users may export their data at any time via their account dashboard in CSV or JSON format.

We access platform data only when you request technical support, when required to maintain system functionality, or when required by law.

06

6. How We Share Personal Data

We do not sell or rent personal data. We may share information in limited circumstances:

Service Providers

We work with trusted service providers to operate the platform, including cloud infrastructure providers, email delivery providers, payment processors, security monitoring services, and analytics providers.

These providers operate under Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) and are only permitted to process data on our behalf.

Financial Compliance

In certain situations, information may be shared with financial institutions or payment providers for fraud prevention, anti-money laundering (AML) compliance, and regulatory reporting obligations.

Legal Compliance

We may disclose personal data when required by law enforcement authorities, courts, government agencies, or applicable laws and regulations.

Business Transfers

If Tamperline.us is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, or asset sale, user data may be transferred to the acquiring entity. Users will be notified if such a transfer occurs.

7. Subprocessors

To operate the Services, Tamperline.us may use the following categories of subprocessors:

  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Payment processors (such as Wise, Stripe, or Paddle)
  • Email delivery services
  • Analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics services)
  • Infrastructure monitoring services
  • Customer support tools

These subprocessors process data only for the purposes of providing the Services.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries outside your own, including the United States. Where international data transfers occur, we use safeguards such as:

  • EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Adequacy decisions
  • Other legally approved transfer mechanisms
09

9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Tamperline.us uses cookies and similar technologies to improve the functionality of the Services.

Essential Cookies

Required for login authentication, platform functionality, and security.

Analytics Cookies

Used to understand feature usage and improve the platform, including tools such as Google Analytics.

Preference Cookies

Used to remember dashboard settings, unit preferences, and saved filters.

Users may disable non-essential cookies through their browser settings.

10

10. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the Services.

Active Accounts

Personal data is retained while your subscription remains active.

Deleted Accounts

Personal data is permanently deleted within 90 days of account closure.

Legal Retention Requirements

Certain financial records may be retained for up to 7 years to comply with tax or accounting laws.

11

11. How We Protect Your Information

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • AES-256 encryption for stored data
  • TLS 1.3 encryption for data in transit
  • Secure cloud infrastructure
  • Role-based access control
  • Automated daily backups
  • Security monitoring systems
  • Periodic security audits and vulnerability testing
  • Incident response and breach notification procedures
12

12. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Data Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain types of data processing
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitations on how your data is used
To exercise these rights, contact ravi@tamperline.us.
