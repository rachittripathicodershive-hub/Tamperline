We’re a small, remote-first team building tools that matter for thousands of independent craft businesses. If you love great software, care about local businesses, and want to work somewhere where your work has a real impact — we’d love to hear from you.

01

Why Tamperline.us

We build software we’re proud of, for customers we genuinely care about. Here’s what makes working here different:

  • Remote-first — work from anywhere in the US
  • Small team, big impact — your work ships fast and reaches thousands of breweries
  • Meaningful product — software that genuinely helps small businesses thrive
  • Async-friendly culture — no endless meetings
  • Competitive pay + equity for full-time roles
  • Annual team retreat at a craft brewery (naturally)
02

Open Positions

We’re always looking for talented people who care about craft, quality, and local businesses.

🔍 We don’t have any open positions right now, but we’re always interested in hearing from talented people. Send your resume and a note about what you’d bring to the team to ravi@tamperline.us
📋 Current Openings No current openings · Check back soon or send a speculative application to ravi@tamperline.us
03

Benefits & Perks

We take care of our team. Full-time employees receive a comprehensive benefits package:

  • Fully remote — work from anywhere
  • Health, dental & vision insurance (US employees)
  • Unlimited PTO with a 2-week minimum
  • Home office setup stipend ($1,000 first year)
  • Learning & development budget ($500/year)
  • Monthly craft beer / beverage allowance 🍺
  • Annual retreat + team events
04

Our Hiring Process

We keep our hiring process straightforward and respectful of your time. Here’s what to expect:

  • Application review within 1 week
  • Intro call with hiring manager (30 min)
  • Technical or skills assessment (paid if > 2 hours)
  • Team interview round (2–3 conversations)
  • Offer & reference checks
05

Apply

Even if there are no open roles right now, we welcome speculative applications. Tell us who you are, what you’d work on, and why Tamperline.us.
